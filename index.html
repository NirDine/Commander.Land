Build your perfect mana base!

Commander.Land is a Magic: The Gathering (MTG) companion tool for the Commander (EDH) format designed to help players in the deck-building process by streamlining the construction of a reliable mana base. It allows new players and veterans alike a fast and easy way to access the vast array of the best land cards available in their colors.
The tool analyzes your deck and offers suggestions tailored to your specific list for a more efficient mana base. It considers factors such as mana curves, color distribution, and land count to ensure that the deck has the appropriate amount of land and color fixing for optimal performance. Whether you're a new player looking to improve your deck or an experienced player seeking a competitive edge, Commander.Land can help you perfect your mana base and take your game to the next level!

Explore all lands in your colors...

Select the colors of your deck and look through all the lands yourself. As you pick the lands you like, we will keep track of everything else and show you your mana distribution in real time! Once you're done, just copy the list, and voilà! Deck-building has never been so easy!

Let us take care of the hard part!

Give us your decklist. We'll do some definitely-not-witchcraft™ and analyze the whole thing, taking into account mana values, color distribution, and even the lands you've already added! Then, we'll give you recommendations on how many lands and colored sources you should be playing. All that's left is for you to pick them!
